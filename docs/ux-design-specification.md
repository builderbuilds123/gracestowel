## Executive Summary

**Project:** Gracestowel - Transparent Impact
A radical transparency feature set for e-commerce that reveals the true cost breakdown of products (materials, labor, ad spend, etc.) and integrates real-time charity donations (Tree Nation, CanadaHelps) to turn transactions into ethical partnerships.

**Target Users:**
Conscious consumers who value authenticity, want to know where their money goes, and are motivated by direct social impact.

## 2. Core User Experience

### 2.1 Defining Experience

**Core Interaction:**
A small, unobtrusive but discoverable "Transparent Pricing" button/trigger on the Product Detail Page (PDP).
- **Trigger:** Hover (desktop) or Tap (mobile).
- **Feedback:** Opens an enlarged, detailed card showing the cost breakdown (donut/waterfall chart).
- **Deep Dive:** Card includes a link to a full "Transparency Page" for users who want to geek out on the data.

**Platform:** Mobile-First Web (Responsive).

**Emotional Goal:** **Trust & Safety**.
Users should feel:
- *Empowered* ("I know where my money goes")
- *Safe* ("This brand isn't hiding anything")
- *Partnership* ("We are in this together")
